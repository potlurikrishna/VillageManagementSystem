Water Department

About Us

The Water Department is responsible for providing safe and clean drinking water to the residents of our village.

We are committed to ensuring that all households have access to adequate water supply and sanitation services.

Services Offered

  • Regular water supply management
  • Maintenance of water pipelines
  • Water quality testing and assurance
  • Leakage repairs and issue resolution
  • Public awareness campaigns about water conservation

Water Supply Timings

Water supply is available to residents as per the following schedule:

  • Monday to Friday: 6 AM - 10 AM & 5 PM - 8 PM
  • Saturday: 7 AM - 11 AM
  • Sunday: No supply

Please ensure to store sufficient water during supply hours.

Water Tank Cleaning Schedule

The following dates are important for the cleaning of the water tank:

  • Last Cleaning: 15th August 2024
  • Next Scheduled Cleaning: 15th November 2024

Cleaning is done quarterly to ensure the quality of water supplied to residents.
