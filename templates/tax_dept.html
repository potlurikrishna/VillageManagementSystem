Tax Department

Property Tax

Description: Tax levied on properties based on their valuation.

Last Date for Payment: 31st March of every financial year

Late Fee: 2% of the outstanding amount per month

Water Tax

Description: Charges for the water supply provided by the village.

Last Date for Payment: 30th June every year

Late Fee: 1% of the outstanding amount per month

House Tax

Description: Annual tax levied on houses based on the area and usage.

Last Date for Payment: 31st December every year

Late Fee: 3% of the outstanding amount per month
