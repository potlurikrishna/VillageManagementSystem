Village Services

Our village administration offers a variety of essential services to all residents. Below is a list of services available to you:

Healthcare Services

We offer basic healthcare services including regular medical check-ups, vaccinations, emergency care, and health awareness programs to ensure the well-being of all residents.

Education Assistance

Our education program provides financial assistance, school supplies, scholarships, and support for students pursuing higher education, ensuring that every child has access to quality education.

Agriculture Support

The village provides agricultural support by offering subsidized seeds, fertilizers, and guidance on crop management, helping farmers to increase yield and improve productivity.

Water Supply

We ensure that clean drinking water is available to all residents. Regular maintenance of water resources and pipes ensures a constant, reliable supply of water throughout the village.

Social Welfare

Our social welfare services are aimed at supporting underprivileged families with financial aid, food distribution, and other basic necessities to help them lead a better life.

Housing Assistance

The village administration provides affordable housing options for families in need. Subsidized housing programs help ensure that every resident has access to safe and secure shelter.

Roads and Infrastructure

We maintain and improve village roads, ensuring safe transportation for residents. Regular repairs and upgrades to infrastructure, including drainage systems, are carried out to ensure the village's smooth operation.

Electricity and Power Supply

The village administration works to provide uninterrupted power supply and ensures proper maintenance of the electrical grid, offering electricity to homes, street lights, and other public services.

Waste Management

Our waste management program ensures proper collection and disposal of waste, promoting cleanliness and hygiene in the village. We also encourage recycling and waste segregation practices.
